01 — Who I Am

Counsel Built on
History & Justice

Advocate, Coimbatore

Born and raised in Krishnagiri, Tamil Nadu, Harshitha S. is a young, determined legal professional whose journey began with a passion for history and a conviction that law is the most powerful instrument of social change.

Her academic path — from CBSE schooling at Sri Vijay Millennium School, Krishnagiri, to a BA in History at Vivekanandha College of Arts & Science for Women, and then an LLB — reflects disciplined curiosity about society, governance, and justice across time.

Now 25, she is among Coimbatore's youngest practising advocates — having commenced active courtroom advocacy with 2 months of hands-on practice at the Coimbatore courts — while simultaneously pursuing her Master of Laws (LLM) at Coimbatore Government Law College.

Harshitha believes that the greatest lawyers are first great students of human nature, history, and context — and she lives that philosophy every day.
